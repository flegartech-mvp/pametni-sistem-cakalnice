## LinkedIn

Waiting rooms still run on paper slips and shouted names. I built Smart Queue System to fix that.

It's a complete queue management interface for clinics, reception desks, and public service offices — built in React 18, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. Staff check in patients and call the next person with one click. A dedicated display view runs on the waiting room TV and updates automatically. Patients scan a QR code at check-in and see their queue position on their own phone without downloading anything.

The entire thing runs in a browser with no backend required. Role-based demo auth gives you separate views for staff, display screens, and admins out of the box. Vitest unit tests and Playwright end-to-end tests are included. CSV export for reporting. Room and department management built in.

If you work in healthcare IT, reception management, or public services — or if you're a developer who wants a serious TypeScript/React reference project — this is worth a look. Repo in comments.
