# PametniSistemCakalnice — 30-Second Video Script

## Hook (0–5s)

**[Screen: a waiting room with paper number slips]**
Narrator (direct, slightly dry): "This is how most clinics manage their queue in 2025."

**[Cut to: the app's staff panel, clean and modern]**
Narrator: "Here's what it looks like when you actually build something."

---

## Show (5–20s)

**[Screen: staff role login, then staff dashboard]**
Narrator: "Staff check in a new patient — they land at the bottom of the queue."
Screen shows patient name appearing in the queue list with a ticket number.

**[Screen: "Call Next" button clicked]**
Queue updates. Current patient highlighted. Ticket number advances.

**[Screen: split view — staff panel left, public display right]**
Narrator: "The waiting room display updates at the same time."
Display screen shows large text: "Now serving: Ticket 47 — Room 3."

**[Screen: phone screen showing QR patient status page]**
Narrator: "And the patient checks their position on their own phone — no app, no account."
Phone shows: "You are number 3 in the queue. Estimated wait: 12 minutes."

**[Screen: admin panel — department settings, CSV export button]**
Narrator: "Admin panel for settings and reports. CSV export included."

---

## Close (20–30s)

**[Screen: GitHub repo]**
Narrator: "React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind. No backend. Fully browser-based. Playwright tested."

**[Text overlay: repo URL]**
Narrator: "Open source. Link below."

**[End card with repo URL and "Star on GitHub"]**
